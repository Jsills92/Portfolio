KOBE BEAN BRYANT

Black Mamba, Vino, The GREATEST Laker of all-time!

Kobe's Life:

  • 1978 - Born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania to Pamela & Joe Bryant.
  • 1989 - Works at his father's games as a ball & mop boy. During halftime, he would practice shooting, often times, turning it into the "Kobe Show".
  • 1991 - Bryant family moves back to Philadelphia. Kobe enjoys his 8th grade year at Bala Cynwyd Middle School.
  • 1992-1996 - Kobe attends Lower Merion High School in Ardmore. His time at Lower Merion starts his national recognition as he earns Pennsylvania's Player of the Year as a junior.
  • 1996 - Drafted right out of high school by the Charlotte Hornets who had traded their pick to the Lakers a day before the draft.
  • 1996-1997 - Bryant becomes the second youngest player to play in an NBA game (18 years, 72 days). Also, became the youngest NBA Slam Dunk contest winner.
  • 1997-1998 - Youngest NBA All-start starter.
  • 1998-1999 - Bryant earns a starting position and a 6-year 70 million contract extension, but the Lakers are swept in the Western Conference Semifinals by the San Antonio Spurs.
  • 1999-2002 - Lakers 3-PEAT! Kobe becomes the youngest player to win 3 championships. He is still seeking his first Finals MVP.
  • 2002-2004 - Lakers keep coming up short in their championship expectations. In 02-03, they lose to the Spurs in WC Semifinals. In 03-04, they lose to the Pistons in the Finals. Shaq leaves for Miami. Phil Jackson's contract was not renewed. Kobe flirts with the idea of signing with the Clippers.
  • 2004-2007 - In 04-05, Kobe was second in scoring, but the Lakers, miss the playoffs. In 05-06, Kobe has an EIGHTY-ONE point game. He goes on to win the league's scoring title but the Lakers fall to the Suns. In 06-07, Bryant records a stretch of four straigh 50-point games. Lakers are once again bumped out by the Suns.
  • 2008-2010 - In 07-08, Kobe wins his first and only MVP. Lakers lose in the finals to the Celtics. In 08-09, the Lakers return to the finals and beat the Orlando Magic in five games. Bryant win his first finals MVP. In 09-10, the Lakers return to their third straight finals. The Lakers redeem themselves by defeating the Celtics in 7 games. Kobe wins his second straight finals MVP.
  • 2010-2016 - The Lakers reach the playoffs in 2011 and 2012, but are booted by the Mavericks and Thunder. That was the last time Kobe played in the playoffs. In 2013, Kobe tears his achilles, days before the playoffs start. Six games into his retun of the 13-14 season, Kobe fractures his knee and is shut down for the season. In 14-15, he tears the rotator cuff in his right shoulder ending his season short. In 15-16, Kobe plays in his 20th & final season for the Los Angeles Lakers. His final game saw him score 60 points against the Utah Jazz.
  • 2018 - Kobe & his film "Dear Basketball" win an Academy Award for Best Animated Shirt Film.
  • 2020 - Kobe, his daughter Gigi, & seven others are tragically lost in a helicopter crash. They were on their way to a basketball game at Mamba Sports Academy.
